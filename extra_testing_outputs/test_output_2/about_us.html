Our Story

Founded in the heart of Pakistan, Cosmetica was born out of a passion for natural beauty and a commitment to sustainable practices. Our founder, Ayesha Malik, envisioned a brand that not only enhances beauty but also respects the environment and the people who use our products.

From sourcing the finest organic ingredients to employing ethical production methods, every step of our process is meticulously crafted to ensure quality and integrity. Join us on a journey towards healthier skin and a healthier planet.
