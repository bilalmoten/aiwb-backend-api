Our Story

Founded in 2023, new9240 emerged from a shared passion for leveraging technology to enhance mental health support. Our team of experts in artificial intelligence and mental wellness came together with a common goal: to create an accessible and effective therapeutic companion for individuals seeking help.

Through continuous innovation and dedication, we have developed an AI-powered therapist that adapts to your unique needs, providing personalized guidance and support whenever you need it most. Our journey is driven by the belief that mental health care should be accessible to everyone, and we are committed to making that a reality.
