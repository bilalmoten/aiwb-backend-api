Our Story

Founded in 2020, newestq Coffee House was born from a deep love for exceptional coffee and the desire to create a community hub. Our founders, passionate about sustainable sourcing and artisanal roasting, embarked on a journey to serve the finest brews while fostering connections among coffee enthusiasts.

From our humble beginnings in a small storefront, we've grown into a beloved local spot known for our cozy atmosphere, delectable pastries, and friendly baristas. At newestq, every cup tells a story, and we're excited to share ours with you.

Our Mission

  • Sourcing Sustainable Beans: We prioritize ethically sourced coffee beans to support farmers and protect the environment.
  • Fostering Community: newestq serves as a gathering place where friends and neighbors can connect over great coffee.
  • Artisanal Roasting: Our expert roasters craft each batch to highlight the unique flavors of every bean.
  • Excellence in Service: We train our staff to provide friendly, knowledgeable service to enhance your coffee experience.
